Choose Your Specialty & Gain Experience

🔥 Pick the path that interests you most. Each explorer program gives you real-world experience and helps you decide if this career is right for you.

🚒 FIREFIGHTING PATHWAY

Sonoma County Fire Explorer Program

What You'll Do:

  • Weekly training at fire stations
  • Learn foundational fire service skills
  • Work with real firefighters and mentors
✓ Eligibility Requirements:
  • Ages 14-20 (must have completed 8th grade)
  • Minimum 2.5 GPA
  • Attend two initial drills before applying
  • Pass an interview
⚠️ This is a selective program. Write a thoughtful, professional email. First impressions matter!
